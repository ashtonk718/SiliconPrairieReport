About Silicon Prairie News

Silicon Prairie News is an independent media outlet focused on tech, startups, and innovation in the Midwest—particularly Nebraska, Iowa, Kansas, and Missouri.

We believe the next wave of impactful companies won’t just come from the coasts. They're already being built right here in the heartland, often quietly, by smart teams solving real problems. Our goal is to amplify those voices and help connect the ecosystem—from college founders to venture capitalists to rural dev teams.

We cover:

  • Early-stage startups and funding rounds
  • AI and advanced technology trends in the region
  • University research and talent pipelines
  • Public-private tech partnerships and infrastructure updates

Silicon Prairie News is proudly local, fiercely curious, and open to tips, contributors, and collaborations. Reach out to us anytime—we’d love to hear from you.
