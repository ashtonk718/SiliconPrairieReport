Apply to Speak at Heartland Developers Conference by July 31

Published: July 2025 • By Fredrick Johnson

The upcoming Heartland Developers Conference, scheduled for September 2025 in Council Bluffs, is calling for speakers to submit proposals by July 31, 2025.

The conference focuses on key areas including cloud computing, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and software development best practices, aiming to bring together professionals across the Midwest tech community.

Organizers encourage developers, industry experts, and thought leaders to share their knowledge and experience through keynotes, breakout sessions, and workshops.

“We want to spotlight the innovative spirit of Midwest developers and foster meaningful connections,” said conference chair Laura Jenkins. “Whether you’re an established expert or emerging voice, this is a great opportunity to contribute.”

Interested speakers can submit proposals and find more information at the official conference website: heartlanddevconf.com.
