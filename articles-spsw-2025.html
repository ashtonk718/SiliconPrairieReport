Silicon Prairie Startup Week Returns Oct 6–11, 2025

Published: July 2025 • By Josh Davidison

The highly anticipated Silicon Prairie Startup Week is set to return from October 6 to 11, 2025, bringing together entrepreneurs, investors, and innovators from across Nebraska and the surrounding Midwest.

This week-long celebration features a packed schedule of events, including workshops, pitch competitions, networking socials, and keynote presentations. Activities will take place in Omaha, Lincoln, Ashland, and Council Bluffs, reflecting the vibrant regional startup ecosystem.

Organizers emphasize the importance of fostering community and collaboration among startups of all stages, aiming to accelerate growth and visibility for local companies.

“Startup Week is a time to highlight the amazing talent and innovation happening right here in the Silicon Prairie,” said event coordinator Michael Torres. “We’re excited to welcome both new faces and returning participants for another inspiring week.”

For the full schedule and registration details, visit the official website at siliconprairienews.com/events/silicon-prairie-startup-week-2025/.
