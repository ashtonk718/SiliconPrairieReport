Prairie Portrait: Meet Dala, Omaha’s Rising AI Artist

Published: July 2025 • By Josh Davidison

Dala, a Nebraska native and AI artist, is gaining attention for blending technology and creativity to produce stunning digital art pieces that explore themes of nature, identity, and futurism.

Using advanced machine learning algorithms and custom neural networks, Dala’s work challenges traditional notions of art and pushes the boundaries of human-computer collaboration.

Her recent exhibition at Omaha’s Bemis Center for Contemporary Arts showcased a series of generative portraits inspired by the Midwest’s landscapes and cultural heritage.

“I want my art to reflect the intersection of the natural world and emerging technologies,” said Dala. “The Silicon Prairie is a unique place where this fusion can happen authentically.”

Dala also hosts workshops and online tutorials, helping other creatives explore AI tools for their own artistic projects.

Follow her work on Instagram: instagram.com/dala_ai_art.
