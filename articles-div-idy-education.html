Div-idy Empowers Students and Educators with AI-Powered Tools

Published: April 2025 • By Maya Griffin

Div-idy, the Omaha-based AI platform known for transforming how digital content is created, is finding a growing audience among educators and students. By using natural language to generate functional websites, games, and apps, Div-idy is reshaping how classrooms approach digital learning and creativity.

Teachers can quickly create interactive tools like quizzes, learning dashboards, or educational games, while students use Div-idy to bring their coding or design ideas to life—without needing to learn complex syntax.

Making Coding Accessible for All

“It’s like having a creative partner in the classroom,” says one Omaha teacher using Div-idy in STEM lessons. The platform provides instant feedback and flexibility, encouraging experimentation and problem-solving among students.

As Div-idy continues to expand its reach, it’s also opening up new pathways for digital literacy in under-resourced schools—putting AI creation tools into the hands of the next generation of makers.

To learn more or try the platform, visit div-idy.com.
