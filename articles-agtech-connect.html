AgTech Connect Conference Set for September 15–17, 2025

Published: July 2025 • By Fredrick Johnson

The annual AgTech Connect Conference will be held September 15–17, 2025, in Lincoln, Nebraska, bringing together agriculture innovators, technology developers, and investors from across the Midwest.

This year’s agenda focuses on sustainable farming technologies, precision agriculture, AI-powered crop management, and the future of food supply chains.

Keynote speakers include Dr. Susan Moreno, a leader in AI applications for crop yield optimization, and Jake Evans, CEO of FarmSense, a Nebraska-based startup pioneering drone-based field monitoring.

The conference also offers workshops, startup pitch competitions, and networking events designed to foster collaboration and investment in agtech innovations.

Registration is open now at the official website: agtechconnect.org.
