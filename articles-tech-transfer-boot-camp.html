UNeMed Hosts Tech Transfer Boot Camp, Aug 18–22

Published: July 2025 • By Josh Davidison

UNeMed, the technology commercialization arm of the University of Nebraska Medical Center, is hosting a week-long Tech Transfer Boot Camp from August 18 to 22, 2025, at the Catalyst building in Omaha.

The boot camp offers hands-on training for researchers, entrepreneurs, and innovators interested in commercializing academic research. Sessions will cover intellectual property, licensing, startup formation, and funding strategies.

Applications are open until July 11, and spots are limited. Participants will gain valuable insights into navigating the path from lab discoveries to market-ready products.

“This program is designed to empower innovators to bring their ideas to life and create meaningful impact,” said Dr. Karen Mitchell, Director of UNeMed. “We’re excited to support the next generation of Nebraska entrepreneurs.”

For more information and to apply, visit the official UNeMed page: unemed.com.
