NMotion Spring 2025 Cohort Welcomes Six Startups

Published: May 2025 • By Jessica Lappen

The NMotion accelerator has announced the successful completion of its Spring 2025 cohort, welcoming six innovative startups from Nebraska and the surrounding region.

The cohort included four Nebraska-based companies such as Kool Kapz, a sustainable packaging startup, and Leadfoot Analytics, specializing in data-driven decision tools for small businesses.

Over 12 weeks, the startups received mentorship, investor connections, and hands-on workshops designed to accelerate their growth and prepare them for the next stage of fundraising.

“It’s inspiring to see these startups harness local resources and make meaningful progress,” said NMotion Program Manager Sarah Kim. “Our goal is to build a thriving ecosystem where founders can grow and succeed.”

The accelerator plans to open applications for its Fall 2025 cohort soon, continuing its mission to empower the Midwest’s most promising startups.

Learn more about NMotion and the current cohort at nmotionaccelerator.com.
