NMotion Accelerator Fall 2025 Cohort Applications Open

Published: July 2025 • By Jessica Lappen

The NMotion accelerator program has officially opened applications for its Fall 2025 cohort, inviting early-stage startups from Nebraska and the surrounding region to apply by August 17, 2025.

Powered by gener8tor, NMotion offers a 12-week intensive program designed to help startups accelerate their growth through mentorship, funding opportunities, and access to a strong regional network of investors and industry experts.

“Our Fall cohort will focus on supporting innovative companies across diverse sectors including software, healthcare, and agriculture technology,” said Sarah Kim, NMotion Program Manager. “We’re looking for passionate founders who are ready to scale and make an impact.”

The program culminates with a Demo Day event where startups pitch to a curated audience of investors, community leaders, and corporate partners.

Interested startups can learn more and submit their applications at the official NMotion website: nmotionaccelerator.com.
