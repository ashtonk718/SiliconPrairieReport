Maha Festival 2025 Returns with Expanded Tech Programming

Published: July 2025 • By Fredrick Johnson

The annual Maha Festival, Omaha’s premier music and arts event, is back for 2025 with a new focus on technology and innovation programming integrated throughout the festival.

This year, attendees can explore interactive tech exhibits, attend panel discussions on AI and creative industries, and participate in workshops featuring local startups and innovators.

Organizers say the expanded tech programming aims to highlight the growing intersection of art and technology in the Silicon Prairie region.

“Maha is about celebrating creativity in all its forms, and this year we’re excited to showcase how tech is shaping the future of art and culture here,” said festival director Jasmine Patel.

The festival runs September 10–14, with main stages located downtown and satellite events across the city.

Learn more and get tickets at the official site: mahafestival.com.
